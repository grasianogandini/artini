About this plan

A home designed
around living

The Canopy Residence was conceived around a central biophilic courtyard that brings natural light and ventilation into the heart of the home. The open-plan layout creates seamless flow between social spaces, while bedroom wings are oriented for morning light and natural cross-ventilation — eliminating the need for air conditioning in mild tropical conditions.

Every proportion and spatial sequence in this plan has been reviewed through a neuroarchitecture lens — ceiling heights that feel instinctively generous, threshold transitions that create psychological comfort, and material recommendations that reduce ambient stress response.
